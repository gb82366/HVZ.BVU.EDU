Mission 4: Look Buford, a Thermonuclear Bomb!

Guys, I have terrible news.

Everyone from the Intergalactic Peacekeepers Society has been taken to the alien mothership! I can only imagine what horrors await them up their... may their efforts to protect us never be forgotten.

Even more troublingly, my instruments are picking up the presence of several thermonuclear bombs hidden around campus! It appears that the aliens want to blow us to smithereens. I fear that if even one of these bombs is detonated, we are doomed!

I am picking up the presence of six of these bombs, but my sensors are not fine-tuned enough to give you exact locations. Only the aliens know where they are. Find them and bring them back to my laboratory in the ESSC Fish Room!

In terror,

Phoebe

This mission will end when at 3:00 or when the Humans complete their objective.

This mission will incorporate the following special rules:

Thermonuclear Bombs. These are objects clearly marked with the radiation symbol that the humans must retrieve. If you are a zombie, you MAY NOT TAKE or TOUCH these objects, but MAY guard them from nearby.
