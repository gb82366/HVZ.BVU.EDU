Mission 7: All Hail David Bowie

Rise and shine!

I am nothing if I am not a student of science. I have long trusted in it, studied it, believed that it would be the savior of humanity. However, in desperate times, I must come to terms with the fact that, sometimes, my beloved discipline has its own limitations.

Sometimes you gotta take some advice from the man livin' on Mars.

Sometimes, you gotta get to church on time.

Here is her plan: she has blessings to spread throughout seven academic buildings on campus. Once all of these blessings are in place, we can call her mission a success!

Meet her at the Admissions Office in DE. Her knowledge of alien religions and philosophy will surely lead us to victory!

In Yoba we trust,

Phoebe
